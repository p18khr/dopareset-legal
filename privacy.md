# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026

DopaReset ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the DopaReset mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Email and Authentication Data**: Your email address and password (securely handled by Firebase Authentication)
- **Usage Data**: Task completions, urge logs, streak information, mood entries, badge progress
- **Optional Data**: Profile information, preferences, feedback

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, app version
- **Analytics Data**: How you use the app (screens visited, features used, duration)
- **Crash Reports**: Error logs and stack traces via Sentry

### 1.3 Cookies & Local Storage

We use AsyncStorage (on-device storage) to:
- Remember your login session
- Store app preferences
- Cache daily content (quotes, quests)
- Track viewing history

No cookies are sent to third parties.
